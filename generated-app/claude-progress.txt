### Key CI/CD Fix
The main challenge was getting esbuild bundling to work in CI.
- Problem: CDK runs `npx --no-install esbuild` from projectRoot (generated-app/)
  but npm ci only runs in infrastructure/
- Solution: beforeBundling hooks copy esbuild, @canopy/shared, and zod
  from infrastructure/node_modules to projectRoot/node_modules
- Added esbuild and zod as explicit infrastructure dependencies
